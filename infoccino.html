Infoccino

Logo

Logo created for the Infoccino website. Digital platform where designers can sell infographics.

The concept of the Infoccino logo is based on pie chart, a prominent graphic that can be found in many infographics today. Every single part of a pie symbolizes creative individuals, who gather together to create a digital platform where through the visualized information they help others to quickly assimilate data, save time and have fun during learning process. Exclamation mark is to indicate high value of the gathered information as well as to show that every individual is important to them. Whether it is a designer who sells his work through the platform or user looking for high quality infographics.
