KillerPixels

Logo and branding

Logo created for the KillerPixels college group of six students: Maija - project manager, Agi - digital marketer, David & Barbara - designers, and two coders, Barry & Garet

Their characteristics:

strong, professional, passionate, individualists, unique, different, creative, sharp, goal cantered, confident, direct, success orientated, experienced.

Design proposal:

Sharp edges - Highlights strengths of the group: self confidence, professionalism, success orientation,

Strong colours - Highlights strengths of the group. Each individual will get different colour which will describe them and reflect such characteristics as: individuality, diversity, experience in different fields. Those colour will show up on their individual parts of the team brand, like business cards, shirts for the final presentation, etc.
